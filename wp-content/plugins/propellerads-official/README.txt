This plugin enables WordPress site owners (Publishers) to easily integrate and manage PropellerAds ad codes to increase revenue from their sites.

== Description ==

PropellerAds is one of the world’s biggest advertising networks, helping website owners, traffic sources, and networks monetize their global mobile and web inventory.

At PropellerAds, we offer a variety of top-performing monetization tools including MultiTag, Push Notification, In-Page Push, Interstitial, Direct Links, and OnClick Popunder.

Our official WordPress Plugin offers three top-performing ad formats - **Onclick, Push Notifications and Interstitials**. The other formats can be integrated manually.

PropellerAds plugin enables WordPress site owners to increase revenue from their sites or blogs easily. You can start monetizing your audience in a few easy steps:
- [Sign-up](https://publishers.propellerads.com/) for a Publisher account.
- Create an ad zone in your PropellerAds Publisher account.
- Install & activate the plugin in your WP admin panel (click "connect to PropellerAds SSP")
- Click the “Update zone list” button to get the active ad zones from your account.
- If you have more than one ad tag, all your ad zones will be loaded automatically and can be found inside the plugin grouped by ad format type (in the dropdown menu).
- Select the zones you want to enable from the list, tick the checkbox “Allow ads on all pages” and then click the “Save Changes” button. Don’t forget – you need to choose the zone for each ad format you want to use on your website.

contact.us@propellerads.com.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Where can I find my Zone ID? =

Find your Zone IDs, log in to your publisher account, click on Sites, and choose a website you want to display ads for. Zone IDs are located in the column “ID.” If you don’t have Ad Zones yet, create them by clicking the “Add Zone” button.

= What are your traffic and website requirements? =

With a few rare exceptions, we accept all sites regardless of traffic volume. However, we do not accept websites containing adult content, malware, or any other content that violates our [Terms and Conditions](https://help.propellerads.com/en/articles/5299692-the-content-of-the-publisher-s-websites-main-rules).

== Installation ==

1. [Sign up](https://propellerads.com/registration-publisher/) for a Publisher account.
2. Create an ad zone in your PropellerAds Publisher account.
3. Install & activate the plugin in your WP admin panel (click "connect to PropellerAds SSP").
4. Click the “Update zone list” button to get the active ad zones from your account.
4. If you have more than one ad tag, all your ad zones will be loaded automatically and can be found inside the plugin grouped by ad format type (in the dropdown menu).
4. Select the zones you want to enable from the list, tick the checkbox “Allow ads on all pages” and then click the “Save Changes” button. Don’t forget – you need to choose the zone for each ad format you want to use on your website.
